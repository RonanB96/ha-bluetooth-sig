# Remove a Device

This guide explains how to remove a single confirmed device and its associated entities.

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings → Devices & Services**
2. Find **Bluetooth SIG Devices** in your integrations list
3. Locate the device you want to remove
4. Select the device entry
5. Select the three-dot menu (⋮) and choose **Delete**
6. Confirm the deletion

All sensor entities associated with that device are removed immediately.

## Re-discovery

After removal, the device may be re-discovered the next time it broadcasts an advertisement. It will appear again in the **Discovered** section for you to confirm or ignore.

If you do not want the device to reappear, you can dismiss the discovery notification when it appears.
